Historical Background

Introduction

British came in 1608 as traders. Later they ruled India. Independence in 1947, Constitution in 1950.

Company Rule (1773–1858)

Regulating Act 1773 controlled East India Company.

Regulating Act 1773 – Features

  • Governor-General system
  • Bombay & Madras under Bengal
  • Supreme Court in Calcutta
  • No private trade
  • Company under British control
