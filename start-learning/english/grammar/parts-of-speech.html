Noun

Name of person, place, thing, or idea.
Example: Ram, Delhi, Book

Pronoun

Used instead of noun.
Example: He, She, It

Verb

Shows action or state.
Example: Run, Eat, Is

Adjective

Describes noun.
Example: Big, Red, Smart

Adverb

Describes verb/adjective.
Example: Quickly, Very

Preposition

Shows relation.
Example: In, On, Under

Conjunction

Joins words/sentences.
Example: And, But, Or

Interjection

Shows feeling.
Example: Wow!, Oh!, Alas!
